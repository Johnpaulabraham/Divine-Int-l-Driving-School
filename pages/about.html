About Us



Welcome to Divine Int'l Driving School, Accredited by the Federal Road Safety Corps, Founded on the principles of excellence and innovation, we offer a comprehensive range of driving courses designed to meet the needs of all learners. Whether you are a beginner just starting your driving journey, an experienced driver looking to refine your skills, or someone in need of specialized training, we have the right program for you.

We believe that driving is not just a skill, but a responsibility that requires confidence, knowledge, and a commitment to safety. We are more than just a driving school—we are a community dedicated to fostering safe, responsible, and environmentally conscious drivers. We equip every student with the tools they need to become not just competent drivers, but exceptional ones. Join us and discover the Divine difference. Let us help you master the road and drive with confidence.





Our Mission

Our mission is to empower every individual with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to become a safe, responsible, and environmentally conscious driver. We are committed to delivering exceptional driver education through innovative teaching methods, personalized instruction, and a strong emphasis on safety and sustainability. By fostering a culture of excellence, we strive to not only prepare our students for the road ahead but also to contribute to safer communities and a greener planet for future generations.

Our Vision

Our vision at Divine Int'l Driving School is to be a leading driving institution that sets the standard for driver education, safety, and environmental responsibility. We aspire to create a world where every driver is equipped with the skills, knowledge, and mindset needed to navigate the roads confidently and sustainably. Through continuous innovation and a commitment to excellence, we aim to transform driver education, making it more accessible, effective, and impactful for all.
