Frequently Asked Questions

What types of driving courses do you offer?

We offer a variety of driving courses tailored to different needs, including:
Beginner Driver Course Executive Course Private lessons Corporate training courses Advance/Defensive driving course Practical Driver Course Theory Driver Course

What are the requirements to enroll in a driving course?

To enroll in our courses, you must meet the following requirements: • Be at least the minimum age required for the course. • Hold a valid learner's permit or driver's license, as applicable. • Provide necessary identification and documentation upon registration.

How can I book a driving lesson?

You can book a driving lesson by: • Visiting our website and using the online booking system. • Calling our office directly to schedule a lesson. • Emailing us with your preferred date and time.

How long is each driving course?

Course lengths vary depending on the type and level of instruction. Typical courses range from a few weeks to several months. Specific details will be provided upon enrollment.
